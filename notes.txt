Installation.
Install the following:
* Virtual Box
* VirtualBox extension pack
* Enable Intel VT-X or AMD-V
* Vagrant
* Vagrant vbguest
  * `vagrant plugin install vagrant-vbguest`
* Forward 443 to 8443 on your router
	

To reprovision VM:
vagrant destroy
vagrant up

To restart:
vagrant reload

To SSH:
vagrant ssh
